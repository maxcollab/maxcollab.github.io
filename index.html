About Us

MaxCollab was founded in 2019 to operate as a blockchain enterprise that focuses on evangelizing blockchain technology and building enterprise solutions that improve the efficacy of legacy systems.

MaxCollab evolved from TheBlockParty a social impact investment company founded in 2017 and the collaboration of three friends who have 15+ years of combined experience in the cryptocurrency/blockchain space
