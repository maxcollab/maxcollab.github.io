A Blockchain Consulting Company

MaxCollab specializes in merging legacy systems with emerging blockchain technology through advocacy, consulting, education and strategic partnerships.

About Us

MaxCollab was founded in 2019 to operate as a blockchain enterprise that focuses on evangelizing blockchain technology and building strategic partnerships between disparate organizations for more robust blockchain adoption.

MaxCollab evolved from TheBlockParty, a social impact investment company founded in 2017, when the original founders acknowledged a need for a more holistic approach to driving adoption of blockchain and other emerging technologies for social impact.
